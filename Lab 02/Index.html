The Best Nepali Momo

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Round dumpling wrappers

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ground chicken, chopped cabbage, onions, cilantro, minced garlic, grated ginger, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Take a dumpling wrapper and place a spoonful of the chicken mixture in the center.
  3. Wet the edges of the wrapper with water and fold it in half, sealing the edges to form a half-moon shape.
  4. Repeat the process with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  5. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with a side of Nepali dipping sauce.

This is my favorite recipe because Nepali Momos remind me of the delicious dumplings when i was in nepal. They are a perfect blend of flavors and the dipping sauce adds an extra kick.
